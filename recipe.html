Teriyaki Chicken with Rice

Ingredients

  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Chicken
  • Rice

Servings

4

Calories

2100

Type

Dinner

Price

$$

Instructions

  • 1) Begin boiling rice
  • 2) Cook chicken on medium high heat for 5 minutes
  • 3) Add teriyaki sauce to chicken and turn heat down to low for 3 minutes
